Brain PC & XREAL Glasses

How the headset hardware actually runs the show: the XREAL glasses display and track, while a host “brain” PC does the thinking — running the Unity build, holding the synced assets, and receiving cues.

🚧

Working understanding. This page is our current best picture, assembled from what we know so far. Items marked (to confirm) still need verifying against the real Unity project and rig — see Verifying this.

Brain PC

The two halves

XREAL glasses are display-and-sensor devices — they show images and report head movement, but they don't run the experience on their own. The experience runs on a host machine, the brain PC.

Brain PC

What moves where

Four flows run through the brain PC during a show.

🖼️

Render & tracking

The brain renders the scene and sends it to the glasses; the glasses send head pose back so content stays anchored.

🎬

Cues

The operator fires cues over OSC; the Unity build on the brain receives them and runs the moment. See OSC control system.

📦

Asset sync

New or changed assets are synced onto the brain so its build has the latest content.

🛠️

Management

Terminal access to start/stop the build, check status, or move files.

Brain PC

Current understanding

As best we know today, two things flow between the brain and the rest of the system:

  1. Asset syncing — project assets are kept in sync onto the brain PC so the build it runs is up to date.
  2. A cue system to the glasses — cues are sent (over OSC) to the Unity build on the brain, which drives what the glasses show.
🧩

That's the skeleton. The details below are what we still need to pin down before this is a reliable reference.

Open questions

The specifics to verify. Each answer turns a (to confirm) above into a fact.

  • Glasses ↔ brain link — USB-C tether? Anything wireless? Which XREAL model and host mode?
  • One brain per headset, or shared? — how many brain PCs for a performance, and how they relate.
  • How the build runs — is the Unity build launched on the brain manually, on boot, or remotely?
  • How assets sync — which tool, which folders, and is it triggered manually or automatically?
  • How cues reach Unity — does OSC hit the brain's Unity app directly on :7000, or pass through something first?
  • Tracking & anchoring on XREAL — how anchors hold on this hardware (ties to the anchoring priority in Technical priorities).
  • Multi-brain networking — if there are several, how they stay in sync.
